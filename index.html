Algorithm Visualizer

An algorithm visualizer is a dynamic tool designed to illustrate the inner workings of algorithms through interactive and graphical representations. Users can select from a range of algorithms, such as sorting or pathfinding, and observe their step-by-step execution in real-time. Through visual cues, playback controls, and customizable inputs, users gain a deeper understanding of algorithmic concepts and behaviors. These visualizers serve as invaluable educational aids, enabling students, educators, and developers to comprehend complex algorithms with greater clarity and engagement.
